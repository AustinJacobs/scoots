Let's Find You The Perfect Rental

We Are Scoots

Scoots is the leading motor scooter rental company on the island of Cozumel, Mexico. Scoots has two rental locations in Cozumel. One near the Playa del Carmen-Cozumel Ferry dock, and near the Terminal Puerta Maya.

Which Rental Is Right For You?

Scooters

Our favorite type of transport. Scooters in Cozumel are the speediest and most sought after mode of transportation. Scooter will save you on gas and get you just about anywhere you need to go. They are also environmentally friendly!

ATV's

What you get is right in the name. This rugged all terrain vehicle will get you through anything you want. ATV's in Cozumel are restricted to dirt roads only, so say chau to the city lights and bustling highways. We recommend renting ATV's if your stay is far from the city.

Jeeps

What can't you do in a Jeep? Scoot's Jeeps are decked out and ready for any adventure. Both street and trail legal, Jeeps are a great rental option for the all around adventurer. Jeep's are the perfect way to spend time in both the city and country, but beware of the fuel economy.
